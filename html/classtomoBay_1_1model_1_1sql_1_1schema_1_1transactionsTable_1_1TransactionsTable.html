Detailed Description

This represents the ebay_transactions table in the database, An order usually consists of one transaction but there are occaisions where a customer will order multiple listings in one order when this is the case there will be multiple transactions associated with a particular order.

It is worth noting that in this scenario there will also be multiple sales record numbers for the order (hence why the sales record numbers may have breaks between orders). It is also worth noting that in the case where there are multiple transactions on an order; there orderID will be a single (large number) rather than a combination of the transactionID and the itemID with a hyphen between them.
