Test:
this class is a unit test case for the RoyalMailNonce class, it makes sure that the integer nonce produced is in Base64 encoding and that the neither the Base64 String nor the integer value repeat within reasonable bounds(10000 iterations)
Precondition
  • none
Postcondition
  • a non repeating (within at least 10000 iterations) integer nonce is produced
  • the nonce is encoded in Base64 encoding.
