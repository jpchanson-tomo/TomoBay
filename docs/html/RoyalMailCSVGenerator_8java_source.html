28 /**
29  * This class represents an object that generates CSV files in a format that the Royal Mail Dispatch
30  * Manager Online can accept.
31  *
32  * The Columns in this CSV are laid out as follows:
33  *
34  * |Field Name |Order|Format|Notes |
35  * |:------------------:|:---:|:----:|:--------------------------------------------------:|
36  * |Service Reference | 0 | n/a | SR1 |
37  * |Service | 1 | n/a | available options: 24/48/SD500/SD1000/SD2500 |
38  * |Recipient | 2 | n/a | recipients name |
39  * |Address Line 1 | 3 | n/a | first address line |
40  * |Address Line 2 | 4 |` n/a | second address line |
41  * |PostCode | 5 | n/a | recipients post code (must match with post town) |
42  * |Post Town | 6 | n/a | recipients town must match with postcode |
43  * |Country Code | 7 | n/a | GB |
44  * |Reference | 8 | n/a | (C or F or P) followed by invoice# |
45  * |Items | 9 |` n/a | always 1 |
46  * |Weight | 10 |12000 | the weight in grams of the invoice |
47  * |Service Enhancement | 11 | n/a | SF (if signed for) OR leave blank |
48  * |Service Format | 12 | n/a | L (for letter) or P(for parcel) |
49  *
50  * @author Jan P.C. Hanson
51  *
52  */
