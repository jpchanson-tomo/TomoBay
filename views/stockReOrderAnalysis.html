Stock Re-Order Analysis

A list of parts that should probably be re-ordered soon as they are below a threshold level

results go here

The system decides what parts are low by working out how much stock is needed for a weeks operation by looking at the sales over the past month. Once the stock for a particular part dips below a pre-set threshold (currently 2 days) the system will advise you to order a particular amount of stock for a part in order to bring the stock level back up to one weeks supply.
